About Random Names Wheel Generator

Random Names Wheel Generator is a free online tool designed to help people make random selections quickly and fairly. Whether you're choosing a giveaway winner, selecting students in a classroom, organizing games, or making everyday decisions, our colorful wheel makes the process simple and enjoyable.

Our goal is to provide a fast, privacy-friendly, and easy-to-use random name picker that works on all devices without requiring registration.
