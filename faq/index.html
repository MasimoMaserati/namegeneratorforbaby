Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Baby Name Generator free?

Yes. Our Baby Name Generator Wheel is completely free to use.

Can I add my own baby names?

Yes. Type your own names and click Add to include them on the wheel.

Is every spin random?

Yes. Every spin is generated randomly, giving each name an equal chance of being selected.

Can I use the generator on my phone?

Yes. The website is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktop computers.

Which baby name categories are available?

You can choose from popular boy names, girl names, Irish, Japanese, Biblical, Nature, Royal, Unique, Italian, Arabic, Nordic and many more.

Do you store the names I enter?

No. Everything happens in your browser. We do not store your personal name lists.
